Our service keeps information about your site links, notifies you about changes and problems with them.

How it works?

Step 1

Enter URL of the page you want to check links on.

Step 2

Linky finds all of the links contained on this page.

when done you can create a new account or add the results to an existing account to save your data.

Step 3

That's all. Now your page is saved to your account and Linky keeps an eye on it :-)

Step 4, not required

In case there are just TOO MANY links on your page, you can check only some of the link ltypes (e.g. web pages, images, css files) or carry out the checks less often.
